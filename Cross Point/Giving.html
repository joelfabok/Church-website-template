Giving

What Does It Look Like?

Your contributions have done some powerful things at Crosspointe Life, and we’re so grateful for your generosity. That’s why we’re excited to let you know about a new way to give that is quick, easy, and goes with you wherever you go: Pushpay.

Pushpay is a great way to give because it simplifies the giving process and allows you to create an account and make a contribution in just 30 seconds. Those of you who prefer to set up scheduled gifts can do that quickly and easily with Pushpay, too.

We’ll be making the church-wide transition to Pushpay soon, so if you’re using CCB to give, you’ll want to start getting familiar with Pushpay.

If you’d like to learn more about Pushpay, including how to get started, how to set up recurring gifts, and how to view your contribution statement, explore this page’s FAQs.

  1. Decide whether this is a one-time gift or recurring gift.
  2. Enter the amount you’d like to give.
  3. Choose the giving type.
  4. Enter your name, email, and phone number (optional).
  5. Choose “Next” and you’ll be prompted to enter some financial
    information. When you’re done, choose “Give” and you’re all set!

How do I manage my account or update my information?

Visit Pushpay’s login page online or on your mobile device and enter your credentials. Here you can view your dashboard, which provides a snapshot into your giving with Pushpay, your transaction history, your security (which includes password change options and authenticated devices), your payment methods, and your profile.

Will I be able to see a summary of my giving with Pushpay?

Yes, login to Pushpay and click on the Transactions menu item to see your transactions through Pushpay.
