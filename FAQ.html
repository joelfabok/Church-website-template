Crosspointe Life's Sunday services begin with an energetic and engaging worship experience. Feel free to sit, kneel, stand, clap, dance or lift your hands during the worship time, knowing that worship is about connecting with God, and not external appearances.

Please be sure to take advantage of our special parking spaces reserved for first time and returning guests. They are located right in front of the main church auditorium. (You will see the signs.) Parking is also available in three separate parking lots. One is located just to the south of our main auditorium on the east side of La Mesa Blvd; our second one is our west parking lot located directly across from the main auditorium; lastly there is parking directly behind the church.

Our goal from the begining has been to create a fun safe environment here at Crosspointe where you are free to come dressed as you feel comfortable and are free to explore your faith in a community of believers and seekers of Kingdom mysteries.

We have an awesome kids program here at Crosspointe for infants to grade 6. Our team is always aspiring to create a safe and fun environment for your children as well as giving them a place to encounter God.

Aroma Cafe is our coffee shop located on campus so that you can come to church and grab a great cup of Jo before or after service. We are also building a growing inventory of books and music that can be purchased as well.

Crosspointe Central is our “hub” for you to connect with whats going on here at Crosspointe! We have hosts that will help you if you need any information on the church or need to sign up for future events. Its also our connection point for our first time guests as well! So make sure you stop by so you can receive your free gift, a book of your choosing.
